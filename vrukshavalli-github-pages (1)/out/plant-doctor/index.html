Common Plant Issues & Solutions

Yellow Leaves

Usually overwatering. Check soil moisture and reduce watering frequency.

Brown Leaf Tips

Low humidity or fluoride in water. Use filtered water and increase humidity.

Drooping Plant

Either under or overwatered. Check soil - if dry, water; if wet, improve drainage.

Slow Growth

Needs more light or nutrients. Move to brighter spot and fertilize monthly.

Pest Problems

Use neem oil spray. Apply weekly until pests are gone.

Root Rot

Remove from soil, trim black roots, repot in fresh, well-draining soil.

Watering Guide

Most plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Check soil moisture with your finger.

Light Requirements

Bright, indirect light works for most houseplants. Direct sun can scorch leaves.

Fertilizing

Feed monthly during growing season (March-October) with balanced liquid fertilizer.
